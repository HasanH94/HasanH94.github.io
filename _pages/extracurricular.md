## Center for Civic Engagement and Community Service (CCECS), AUB
_Volunteer · Aug 2021 – present_

AUB's CCECS runs the university's civic-engagement programs with local and international NGOs. I've contributed roughly **30+ hours every semester** since enrollment.

- **Beirut Port Blast rebuild.** Led and participated in the clearing, transport, and repair effort across approximately **40 homes** damaged by the August 2020 explosion — debris removal, moving construction materials, and repainting walls.
- **Tutoring.** Tutored 8th-9th grade students in Math, Physics, and English.
- **Community visits.** Organised and joined visits to orphanages and elderly homes.

## American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME) — AUB Chapter
_Social Media Team Leader → Head of Sub-Cabinet Teams · Sept 2021 – Jun 2024_

- Co-managed three sub-teams: **social media**, **professional development**, and **events planning**.
- Oversaw organization and sponsorships for **8+ chapter events**, across recreational, technical, and academic categories.
- Built and maintained the ASME-AUB chapter LinkedIn presence — two-plus weekly posts, speaker outreach (professors, industry engineers, Ph.D. candidates), and event promotion.
- Delivered a chapter website that made university resources accessible to **200+ members**.

## 180 Degrees Consulting — AUB Branch
_Consultant · Sept 2022 – Sept 2023_

180DC is the world's largest university-based consultancy for non-profit clients. I worked on two pro-bono engagements:

- **Marine-reserve expansion (Malaysia).** Advised a Malaysian NGO on growing the country's marine-reserve footprint. Benchmarked against more than five peer Malaysian NGOs and co-developed a volunteering-program proposal informed by the client's operational constraints.
- **Pet-therapy integration (Netherlands).** Advised a Dutch NGO on introducing pet-therapy programs into elderly-care homes — reviewing the existing literature, designing a pilot framework, and outlining implementation requirements.
